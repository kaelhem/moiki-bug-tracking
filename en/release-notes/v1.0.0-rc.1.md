# v1.0.0-rc.1 🎉

Six years of Moiki growing commit after commit. Today, the app feels mature enough to move to **1.0.0** (Release Candidate).

---

## Editor

### [feats]
+ **Series / Episodes**: feature fully audited and stabilized.
+ **Search** in the Social Club.

### [fix]
+ Fixed links to stories containing **emojis**.
+ Fixed **tutorials**.
+ Fixed display issue with overly wide **comments**.
+ Fixed **logout redirection** bug.

---

## Player

### [improvements]
+ **Resilient Howler**: automatic retry (2 attempts) on dynamic import, no more crashes on Mobile Safari.
+ **Reduced blur and aggressive animations** on mobile.

---

## Platform & Backend

### [fix]
+ Fixed **password reset email** issue (sending was broken in some cases).
+ Fixed sending of **comment reply emails**.
+ Fixed **OG tags** (social preview images) on `moiki.fr`.

---

## Infrastructure Migration

Alongside this RC, the infrastructure has been migrated from Hostinger to OVH.
